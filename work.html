Our Work

Discover the impact we're making in communities across Jinja, Uganda through our various programs and initiatives

Empowering Communities Together

Zion at Heart Foundation is dedicated to empowering communities in Jinja, Uganda by providing access to education, healthcare, and vocational training. Our goal is to create sustainable solutions that address poverty, inequality, and social injustices, ultimately improving the lives of those in need.
