About Us

Learn about our mission to empower communities and create positive change in Jinja, Uganda

Empowering Communities Together

At Zion at Heart Foundation, we are dedicated to empowering communities in the Jinja region of Uganda. Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to improve the lives of individuals and families, promote education and healthcare, and create sustainable development opportunities.

Join us in making a positive impact in our community. Together, we can create lasting change and build a brighter future for all.

Our Mission

To empower marginalized communities in Jinja, Uganda by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and sustainable development opportunities that break the cycle of poverty and promote social justice.

Our Vision

A thriving community where every individual has the opportunity to reach their full potential, free from the barriers of poverty, discrimination, and inequality.

Our Values

Compassion

We approach every individual and community with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for their wellbeing.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of partnerships and working together to achieve sustainable, long-lasting change.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of transparency, accountability, and ethical conduct in all our operations.

Empowerment

We focus on building capacity and empowering communities to become self-sufficient and sustainable.
